About

I'm a laid back guy with a good (dry) sense of humor. I can quote the classics (like Dumb and Dumber and Austin Powers) with the best of them. I enjoy playing softball, soccer and golf as well as coaching my son's various sports endeavors. One of these days, when I have time, I'm going to become proficient with working on my '66 mustang that's sitting in the garage waiting.

I truly enjoy being a developer and the ability to work on a product excites me. I love the freedom a product gives you to add features you want, as well as how it allows you to drive the direction of the product. I prefer to get my hands dirty in the code and having the ability to learn new technologies, but I don't mind performing managerial tasks when required.

My personal site, casad.net, is a little peek into me and the things I like. It was built simply to make it quick and easy for me to consolidate my favorite links in one spot.
